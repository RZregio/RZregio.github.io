Backstory

Tokyo was once alive and familiar—until it vanished. Now, a few survivors are trapped in a twisted version of the city, forced into deadly games that test their mind, strength, and will. Each win brings them closer to the truth, but in the Borderland, even answers can kill.
As they fight to survive, a darker mystery unfolds. The deeper they go, the more dangerous it gets. Will they escape, or are they just pawns in a game?

Facts About the Borderland
  • Set in a deserted, altered Tokyo
  • Players must compete in deadly games
  • Each game is tied to a playing card
  • Winning extends your stay—and your life
  • Every victory reveals a piece of the mystery
  • There’s no clear escape, only survival
